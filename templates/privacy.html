Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

BookerAI ("us", "we", or "our") operates the BookerAI platform. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

2. Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information to provide and improve our Service to you.

Types of Data Collected:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number, business/profile information.
  • Booking Data: Details about appointments and schedules.
  • Usage Data: Information on how the Service is accessed and used, including device information, crash logs, and diagnostic data.

3. Use of Data

BookerAI uses the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Service

4. Payment Handling

Payments are processed directly by Stripe, our third-party payment processor. We do not store or collect your payment card details. That information is provided directly to Stripe whose use of your personal information is governed by their Privacy Policy.

5. Communications

We may use your Personal Data to contact you with newsletters, marketing or promotional materials, and other information obtaining to the Service. Service-related notifications (e.g., booking confirmations, password resets) are mandatory.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party companies (specifically Stripe, hosting providers, analytics services) to facilitate our Service.

We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

7. Data Retention

We will retain your Personal Data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy.

8. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

9. Your Data Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete the information we have on you. You can manage your data settings within your account profile or contact support to request deletion.

10. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@bookerai.com.
